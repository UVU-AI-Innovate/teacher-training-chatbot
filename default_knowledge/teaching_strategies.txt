Active Learning Strategies
- Use manipulatives and hands-on materials
- Incorporate movement and gestures
- Create interactive games
- Use visual aids and diagrams
- Ask open-ended questions

Differentiation Techniques
- Break tasks into smaller steps
- Provide multiple ways to solve problems
- Use scaffolding techniques
- Offer choice in activities
- Adjust pacing for individual needs

Positive Reinforcement
- Give specific praise
- Celebrate small victories
- Use growth mindset language
- Acknowledge effort and progress
- Build on student strengths

Attention Management
- Use clear signals for transitions
- Incorporate movement breaks
- Change activities every 15-20 minutes
- Use proximity control
- Implement visual timers
