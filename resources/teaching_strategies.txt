Active Learning Strategies for Math
- Use manipulatives for hands-on learning
- Draw pictures to visualize problems
- Create real-world connections
- Practice with games and activities

Handling Student Frustration
- Break tasks into smaller steps
- Provide immediate positive feedback
- Allow think time
- Use growth mindset language

Attention Management Techniques
- Incorporate movement breaks
- Use visual timers
- Change activities every 15-20 minutes
- Implement silent signals
